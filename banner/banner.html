01
The Problem & Our GoalWhy it matters

In industry, the cost of being late is measured in lives. Most workplaces still rely on cameras that only record what already happened. VisionSafe 360 makes that same infrastructure recognize danger as it unfolds and intervene before the accident occurs — replacing fragmented, reactive tools with continuous, evidence-driven prevention.

02
Value & FeasibilityBuilt for adoption

Nothing to replace. It runs on the CCTV a site already operates. All analysis happens locally at the edge — no cloud-upload costs, data sovereignty satisfied, real-time response independent of the network. Scales linearly per camera, from one device to a multi-site enterprise, aligned with OSHA, NIOSH & ISO 45001.

03
How It WorksFour cohesive layers
Unified AI Safety Intelligence
PPE, proximity, falls & posture monitored together — the central technical contribution.
Real-Time Command Center
Multi-camera dashboard, alert triage, safety zones & full incident lifecycle + mobile push.
Intelligent Decision Support
Detections validated before alerting — eliminating alert fatigue; analytics surface hazard patterns.
Resilient & Accessible
Keeps detecting offline, auto-syncs on reconnect; bilingual EN/AR, fully accessible.
